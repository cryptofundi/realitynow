What is a City Launch Partner?

A City Launch Partner (CLP) is an independent local growth operator who seeds the first community of active RealityNow users in a specific neighbourhood. You are not posting data yourself - you are building the network of real people who will post and validate for years to come.

What you do as a CLP
1
Educate people in your area
Show neighbours, students, shopkeepers how the app works. Explain what RealityNow is and why it is useful for daily decisions.
2
Get them to make one real post
Help each person post their first reality update. This unlocks their Validator status and makes them a genuine active user (VAU).
3
Ensure they validate 5 times
A fully qualified VAU has verified their phone, made 1 valid post, and submitted 5 validations. Guide them to reach this milestone.
4
Build density in one area first
Do not spread thin across a whole city. Pick one high-traffic zone - a college, a tech park, a market - and build deep density there first.
What you do NOT do
X Post fake or duplicate updates yourself
X Pay people per post - this creates spam
X Onboard users who are not real people in your area
X Claim credit for organic signups not from your referral
X Share your referral code with people outside your zone

What is a Verified Active User (VAU)?

A VAU is a user who has done all four of these things:

Phone verified via OTPProves they are a real person with a real number
Made 1 valid postPost confirmed as accurate by community - not just posted
Completed 5 validationsActively participated in confirming others' posts
At least 1 correct validationProved they are validating thoughtfully, not randomly
Why is this definition hard to fake?

Making a "valid post" requires other real validators to confirm it. Validators must be physically nearby to get full weight. You cannot create 50 fake accounts and call them VAUs - the validation system requires real people to be in real locations.
