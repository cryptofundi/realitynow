What is RealityNow?

RealityNow is a real-time truth network. Real people report what is actually happening at locations near them right now - traffic jams, crowded malls, long queues, full parking lots. Other people nearby verify those reports. The result is reliable, live information you can actually trust - posted by your neighbours, confirmed by the community.

Think of it like asking your neighbour "Is the GVK Mall parking full right now?" - except thousands of neighbours are answering, all at once, all the time.
6 categories covered
🚗 Traffic - road congestion
☕ Crowd - cafes, restaurants
🏢 Queue - hospitals, banks
🛒 Mall - shopping centres
🌧 Weather - rain, flooding
🕒 Parking - available spots

How it works in 3 steps
1
Someone posts a reality update
A user is stuck in traffic on PVNR Expressway. They open RealityNow, select Traffic, take a photo, pay Rs.5, and post. The report appears on the feed within seconds with a "New" badge.
2
The community verifies it
Nearby users see the post and tap Confirm or Dispute. At least 3 people must confirm before it becomes "Verified". Votes are hidden from each other to prevent bias.
3
Truth is locked on the blockchain
Once verified, the report is stored permanently on Polygon blockchain. Nobody can delete it. Anyone who opens the app in that area sees accurate, community-confirmed information.

The three roles
📷
Poster
Everyone starts here
A Poster reports what they see at a location. Anyone can post. You pay Rs.5 as a quality deposit - this prevents spam and fake reports. If your post is confirmed by the community as accurate, you earn credits back. If it is rejected, you lose the Rs.5 - this keeps the truth network clean.
Why Rs.5? Free posting leads to spam. The Rs.5 deposit makes people think twice before posting something false. Accurate posters earn it back as credits.
✅
Validator
Unlocked after 1 valid post
A Validator confirms or disputes other people's posts. You look at the photo and GPS location and tap Confirm or Dispute. The community's combined votes determine whether a post becomes "Verified Truth". Validators earn +1 credit for each correct vote.
Important: You cannot validate your own posts. And votes are kept hidden until the window closes so nobody just copies what others voted.
⭐
Trusted User
Earned by reputation score 20+
Trusted Users have proven their accuracy over time. Their validation votes carry 3x more weight than a new user. They get a visible trust badge in the feed. They are the backbone of the truth network. You become Trusted automatically when your reputation score reaches 20.

How to become a Validator

You only need to do one thing: make one post that gets confirmed as valid by the community. That's it. After your first valid post, the Validate tab unlocks automatically.

1
Post your first reality update
Tap the + button. Report something real you can see right now - traffic, crowd, parking. Pay Rs.5.
2
Wait for community confirmation
3 nearby users confirm your post. You earn +2 credits and your reputation score increases.
3
Validator access unlocked
The Validate tab is now active. Start confirming or disputing posts to earn +1 credit per correct vote.
Tip for students and delivery riders: The best way to get your first valid post is to report something obvious - a traffic jam you are sitting in, a crowded bus stop, a full parking lot. Take a clear photo. The community will confirm it quickly and you become a Validator immediately.

What if nobody validates my post?

Your post still shows in the feed - immediately and visibly - with a yellow "New" badge. Here is exactly what happens over time:

Time after posting What happens
0-10 minutes Post shows at top of feed with pulsing yellow "New" badge. System sends validation nudge to nearby users offering +2 credits to be first to verify.
10-30 minutes Post slowly sinks in the feed ranking. Badge changes to orange "Verifying". Still visible but less prominent.
30-60 minutes Post moves to Recent History section. Feed prioritises fresher content. Users can still find it but it no longer drives decisions.
Never reaches 3 validators Outcome is "Inconclusive". Your Rs.5 is used by the platform. You earn 0 credits. Your reputation does not drop - you are not penalised for low validator density in your area.
The good news: An inconclusive outcome does not hurt you. Your post was real - it just did not reach the minimum validator count. This is a signal that your area needs more active validators. A City Launch Partner in your area can solve this over time.

If your score dropped unfairly - how to recover

Sometimes a genuine post gets disputed incorrectly - the situation changed between when you posted and when validators checked, or a small group of biased users voted against it. This does happen. Here is how to recover quickly:

1
Focus on validation, not posting
Correct validation votes give +0.3 score each. 10 correct validations = +3 score. This is the fastest way to recover.
2
Post only when very certain
Choose locations where the situation is obvious and undeniable - a clearly full parking lot, a very long visible queue. Photo quality matters. Clear photos get confirmed faster.
3
Use the Ground Witness bonus
Validate posts at locations where you are physically present. GPS confirms you are nearby. Ground witness votes carry more weight and earn +0.5 extra credits.
The 7-day inactivity decay (5% reduction) is the main enemy of your score. Even 5 minutes of validation per day prevents decay completely.

What the blockchain means for you

RealityNow runs on Polygon blockchain - a public, permanent ledger. Here is what that means in plain language:

1
Every post is permanent
The moment you post, it is written to the blockchain. Nobody can delete it - not even the RealityNow team. A post saying "GVK Mall parking was full at 3pm on 29 April 2026" will exist on the blockchain forever.
2
Every validation is recorded
Who confirmed, who disputed, when, and from where. All on-chain. This makes the validation system tamper-proof. No one can go back and change the votes.
3
Your reputation cannot be faked
Your reputation score is stored on the blockchain, not in a database that someone could edit. Every score change comes from a real post or validation event. It is a genuine track record.
4
If RealityNow shuts down tomorrow
All your posts, all validations, all reputation scores remain on Polygon forever. Anyone can rebuild the app from the blockchain data. The truth is not owned by any company - it belongs to the community that created it.
You do not need to understand blockchain to use RealityNow. Think of it as a public notice board that nobody controls and nobody can erase. The app just makes it easy to read and write to that board.

Frequently asked questions
Why do I have to pay Rs.5 to post? +
The Rs.5 is a quality deposit, not a fee. It prevents spam and fake reports. If your post is confirmed as accurate by the community, you earn credits. If it is rejected as false, the Rs.5 stays with the platform. This small friction keeps the truth network reliable.
What can I actually do with credits? +
Credits can be spent to: boost your posts to the top of the feed (5 credits), highlight posts visually (3 credits), post a help request like "need cook" or "need driver" (3 credits), offer something to nearby users like free tea or a discount (10 credits), set location alerts for saved places (1 credit per month), or promote a local business in a 2km radius (50 credits). Credits cannot be converted to cash.
How many credits can I earn per day? +
The maximum is 10 credits per day from all sources combined. The daily cap prevents the system from being gamed. At cap, you earn: 3 credits for a stale-area gap post, plus 7 correct validation votes (+1 each). A consistent daily contributor earns around 300 credits per month.
My post was marked Invalid. Why? +
It means fewer than 70% of validators agreed with your report. Most common reasons: the situation changed between when you posted and when validators checked (traffic cleared, crowd thinned), or the photo was unclear and validators could not confirm what you described. An invalid post costs you -1.0 reputation score. Focus on posting obvious, easily verifiable situations with clear photos.
Nobody validated my post. Did I lose my Rs.5? +
Yes, but your reputation score does not drop. An Inconclusive outcome (fewer than 3 validators in the window) means the post was not confirmed or denied. It simply expires. This is not your fault - it usually means your area needs more active validators. Your post still appeared in the feed during its fresh window and may have helped people who saw it.
Can someone game the system and hurt my reputation? +
The system has several protections. First, each disputing user must have their own valid post history - brand new wallets cannot remove posts. Second, fast rejection (a post hidden immediately) requires 2 disputes from Contributor-tier users or above. Third, if validators vote wrongly consistently, their own reputation drops and their vote weight decreases. Over time, bad actors are self-eliminated. If you believe your score was affected unfairly, focus on validation to recover quickly.
How do I change my location in the app? +
Tap the location label at the top of the screen (it shows your current area like "Banjara Hills, Hyderabad"). A search panel opens. Type any area name - Madhapur, Kondapur, Jubilee Hills - and tap a result. The feed will instantly show truth from that area. Tap "Use my current location" to go back to your GPS location.
Is my location data private? +
Your exact GPS coordinates are never stored or shared publicly. What goes on the blockchain is a geohash - a short code representing a neighbourhood area (roughly 500m x 500m). Nobody can determine your exact street address from a geohash.
What is a Ground Witness bonus? +
If you are physically within 500 metres of the location you are validating, you get a Ground Witness bonus. Your vote counts 1.5x more than normal, and you earn an extra +0.5 credits. This is the app's way of rewarding people who are actually there - delivery riders, students, commuters - not people validating from their sofa based on a photo alone.
Does this work without internet or GPS? +
Internet is required to load the feed and post. GPS is required for full credit eligibility. If GPS is off, you can enter your pincode instead - but posts made via pincode earn 50% fewer credits and carry less trust weight than GPS-verified posts.
